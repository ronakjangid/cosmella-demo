Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer private-label perfume manufacturing?

Yes, we offer a full contract for private-label perfume manufacturing, including custom scent, packaging, and delivery.

How long is the manufacturing timeline?

As a bulk perfume production company, we maintain a production timeline of 4 to 6 months, covering packaging, artwork and labels, freight, blending & filling, QC, assembly, and final delivery. Rush services are also available.

Do you provide custom perfume formulation?

Yes, we are custom perfume manufacturers, and our experts will work with your preferred direction, notes, and target profile to create unique formulations. All formulas remain proprietary to you, and we can produce them at any scale you need.

Can you match or recreate an existing fragrance?

Yes, we can match or recreate an existing fragrance. Our perfumers analyse the scent profile and key notes, then formulate a close match that is legally compliant and unique to your brand. We provide samples for your approval and can adjust the formula until it meets your expectations.

Are you an OEM perfume manufacturer?

Yes. We are an OEM perfume manufacturer specializing in custom fragrance development and bulk production.

Do you manufacture perfumes for wholesale distribution?

Yes, we work with wholesale perfume manufacturers for bulk orders.

Do you offer fragrance manufacturing for retailers?

Yes. Our services include fragrance manufacturing for retailers developing their own branded collections.
