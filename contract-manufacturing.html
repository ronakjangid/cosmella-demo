Contract Manufacturing Services

Reliable aerosol & fragrance production supported by quality processes and a global sourcing network.

Your vision, produced with technical precision

Brands with their own formulas and packaging rely on us for professional filling, assembly, and logistics. Our production standards, controlled maceration rooms, and calibrated QC systems ensure your fragrance reaches the market with consistency and excellence.

Our manufacturing
capabilities

Controlled maceration

Temperature stable environments for optimal maturation and integration of ingredients.

Automated and semi automated filling

Accurate filling for small batches and large scale volumes.

Crimping and component application

Precise pump and collar application for long term product stability.

Bottle cleaning and inspection

Sanitization, leak tests, and thorough quality checks through multiple control points

Final assembly

Sleeves, boxes, inserts, and sets prepared by specialists to achieve retail ready quality

Coding and documentation

Full traceability and compliance support for international markets.

Ideal for

Established fragrance brands

Brands with proven formulas and market presence seeking a reliable manufacturing partner that delivers consistent quality, regulatory compliance, and scalable production across multiple markets.

Emerging perfume houses

Growing fragrance houses that have completed creative development and require professional-grade production, quality control, and operational support to bring their scents to market with confidence.

Beauty companies with internal creative teams

Organizations that manage formulation and design in-house but need a trusted manufacturing partner to execute filling, assembly, documentation, and logistics to global standards.

Brands that already have formulas and packaging suppliers

Companies looking to integrate their existing formulas and packaging components into a seamless, quality-controlled production process without changing their established creative or sourcing partners.
