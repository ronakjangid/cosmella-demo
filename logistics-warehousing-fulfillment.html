Logistics, Warehousing & Fulfillment

Seamless global logistics for brands that need reliable delivery and operational ease.

Reliable movement from factory to shelf

We handle logistics with the same precision and care applied to aerosol & fragrance creation and manufacturing. Our operations team ensures every product moves safely, efficiently, and compliantly through the supply chain, supporting both brands and large scale retail programs.

Our capabilities

This end to end logistics framework ensures smooth production flow, reduced lead times, and dependable delivery, enabling your brands to scale confidently with a partner who manages all operational complexity behind the scenes

  • Strategic storage and handling of raw materials and finished goods
  • Inventory management and SKU tracking for full stock visibility
  • Global freight coordination for air, sea, and ground shipping with trusted logistics partners
  • Import and export documentation, customs compliance, and regulatory support
  • Fulfillment for direct to consumer, retail, wholesale, and influencer mailers
  • Secure storage, quality controlled handling, and serialized batch tracking
