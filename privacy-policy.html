K Luxury Fragrances Privacy Notice

K Luxury Fragrances (“Company,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Notice explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit https://www.kluxuryfragrances.com/.

1. Information We Collect

a. Information You Voluntarily Provide

When you submit an inquiry or requirement form on our website, we collect the information you choose to provide, which may include:

  • First and last name
  • Business email address
  • Phone number
  • Company name
  • Company website or LinkedIn page
  • Quantity and brand stage selections
  • Service scope selections
  • Any information you include in the “Describe your need” field

This information is collected for the purpose of reviewing your request and responding to your inquiry.

b. Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our website, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Pages visited and interactions with the website
  • Time spent on pages
  • Browser type, device type, and operating system
  • General geographic location (such as city or state)
  • IP address

This information is used in an aggregated manner to understand website usage, improve performance, and maintain website operations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and requests
  • Communicate regarding requested services
  • Improve our website and offerings
  • Maintain website security and functionality

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information.

We may share information only:

  • With service providers that support website operations or communications
  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of the Company or others

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Choices

You may:

  • Choose not to submit information through website forms
  • Contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of information you have provided

6. Children’s Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or our data practices, please contact us at:
