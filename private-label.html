Private Label Fragrance Programs

Create exclusive scents and packaging with a streamlined process built for speed and brand
differentiation.

A fast and effortless way to launch your own fragrance line

Our private label program is designed for retailers and businesses that need beautiful fragrances delivered quickly. You choose from refined formulas, premium stock bottles, and decoration options. We handle the development, filling, finishing, and global delivery.

The result is a fully branded product line that feels custom without long timelines or high complexity

Curated fragrance library

Choose from a selection of pre developed eau de parfum and eau de toilette profiles created by senior perfumers.

Premium packaging choices

Select bottles, caps, and decorations from luxury components sourced worldwide.

Brand adaptation and customization

We integrate your logo, color palette, and brand identity into the packaging and presentation.

Fast production timelines

Stock components allow for production windows of two to five months depending on volumes.

Low MOQs for growing brands

Flexible quantities make it easy to launch test collections or retailer exclusives.

Ideal for

Retailers

Retailers looking to launch exclusive, margin-driven fragrance lines with fast turnaround, low risk, and consistent quality across multiple SKUs.

Lifestyle Brands

Brands seeking to extend their universe into fragrance with a cohesive, on-brand scent offering that strengthens identity and customer loyalty.

Boutiques

Independent boutiques and concept stores wanting small-batch, differentiated fragrances that feel curated, premium, and exclusive to their audience.

Corporations seeking branded gifting

Companies creating high-impact fragrance gifts for clients, employees, or events delivered quickly, fully branded, and produced to premium standards.
